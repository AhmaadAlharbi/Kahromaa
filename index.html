About

KAHROMAA

Established in 2018

At Kahromaa, our mission is to deliver exceptional electrical solutions tailored to the needs of industrial, commercial, and residential sectors. Our team of qualified engineers and electricians provides installation, testing, and maintenance services for medium and low voltage systems. We cater to industrial facilities, large commercial utilities, and residential projects, ensuring complete compliance with safety regulations. Our commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry.

Divisions
Industrial

Our Industrial Division specializes in providing robust electrical solutions for factories, refineries, and other industrial facilities. We also serve as a subcontractor for large-scale industrial projects, offering specialized expertise and support. We understand the unique challenges and requirements of industrial environments and offer customized services to meet these needs.

Commercial

The Commercial Division focuses on delivering efficient electrical systems for shopping malls, office buildings, and large commercial complexes. Our solutions are designed to enhance performance, reduce downtime, and ensure safety.

Residential

Our Residential Division provides comprehensive electrical services for residential buildings, apartment complexes, and housing developments. We ensure that our solutions are safe, reliable, and tailored to the specific requirements of residential projects.

Services
